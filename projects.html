ASTRA / VITALITY

  • ASTRA - Advanced Space Technologies and Research Alliance (MUR - Ministero dell’Università e della Ricerca & PNRR - Piano nazionale di ripresa e resilienza). Period: 2022 - current.
  • Aims at investigating and developing innovative software architectures for new space applications. The idea goes towards a framework that hosts accelerators for artificial intelligence, capable of autonomy on board. This technology should allow maneuvers, reconfigurations, and on-board diagnostics-based artificial intelligence, as well as update of software when the satellite is in orbit.
  • Keywords: space, software architecture, artificial intelligence, devops.

Pauliceia

  • Pauliceia 2.0: Collaborative Mapping of the History of São Paulo (1870-1940) - phase 2. (CNPq - Conselho Nacional de Desenvolvimento Científico e Tecnológico). Period: 2023 - current.
  • The project foresees the availability on the web, a historical digital cartographic base of the city of São Paulo, referring to the period of its urban-industrial modernization (1870-1940). It offers an interface that allows interactivity of interested researchers, so that they can feed the available database with spatializable events from their own investigations. This way, interested researchers will be able to produce maps and visualizations of their respective research. This second phase aims to expand the spatial coverage, the platform's functionalities, the engagement of the target community and the conditions for replicating the project to other cities.
  • Keywords: digital humanities, collaborative systems, volunteered geographic information.
